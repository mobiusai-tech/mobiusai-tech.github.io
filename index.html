Mobius-AI is an early-stage company and pioneer in the field of fleet management for electrified transportation. Born out of Vanderbilt University with research funded through National Science Foundation and Department of Energy, our company is revolutionizing the industry with a reconfigurable artificial intelligence platform for complex online planning and optimization of large vehicular fleets. Our goal is to elevate efficiency, sustainability while reducing uncertainty through the use of non-myopic sequential decision procedures at scale.



Our Products



  • OptiTransit
    Cloud-based system to manage operations of microtransit, paratransit and fixed line bus systems including predictive routing, manifest scheduling, and detailed report generation.
  • FleetCharge
    Patent pending cloud application for planning charger placement, dynamic routing and scheduling of mixed fleet consisting of diesel, hybrid and electric vehicles.
  • Freight IQ
    Dynamic vehicle routing at large scale and integrated tracking and insight into operations at scale for last mile freight operations.
